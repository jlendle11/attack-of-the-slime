INTRO

Recently fallen from space, you play a slime blob who has only two objectives in life. Cover everything in slime, and consume humans.

INSTRUCTIONS

The goal is to cover every accessible tile of the board before consuming the human at the end.
Use the arrow keys to move the blob around the level filling the board as you go.
BEWARE
Your slime trail is toxic. Going over your own trail will ultimately result in a GAME OVER.
Failure to fill the whole board before eating the human will also result in a GAME OVER
